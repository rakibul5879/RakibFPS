RakibFPS is a lightweight, performance-first modpack designed to provide a smoother, faster, and more responsive Minecraft experience. Built specifically for the **Fabric** loader, it acts as a modern, open-source alternative to OptiFine.

Whether you're playing on a low-end laptop or a high-end gaming PC, RakibFPS ensures your hardware is utilized to its full potential.

---

### 🚀 Performance Features
* **Sodium:** A complete overhaul of the rendering engine for massive FPS gains.
* **Lithium:** Optimizes game physics, mob AI, and tick logic without changing gameplay.
* **Starlight:** Rewrites the light engine to make chunk loading and lighting updates lightning-fast.
* **FerriteCore:** Dramatically reduces memory (RAM) usage.

### 💎 The "OptiFine" Experience
Don't lose your favorite features. RakibFPS includes:
* **Shader Support:** Full compatibility with shader packs via Iris Shaders.
* **Zoom:** High-quality cinematic zoom (default key: C).
* **Connected Textures:** Visual parity for glass, sandstone, and more.
* **Better Grass & Snow:** Cleaner looking environments.

### 🛠️ Technical Specifications
* **Loader:** Fabric
* **Server Compatibility:** Fully compatible with Vanilla, Spigot, Paper, and Fabric servers.
